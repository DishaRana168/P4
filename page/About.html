About Me

Hello, I am Disha Rana. I am undergraduate and transfer student attending New Jersey Institute of Technology pursuing a Bachelors Degree in Information Technology with a concentration in Network and Information Security. I also intend to pursue my Master's Degree in Computer Science. After graduation, My goal is to utilize my knowledge of technology and security to help companies develop secure and reliable systems. I am an effective team player who is able to take initiative and work independently on projects. I am also passionate about keeping up with the latest trends and technologies in the IT and cybersecurity industries.

My goal for this webpage is to create a cooking recipe to provide instructions that will help someone to prepare a particular dish or meal. The recipe will include details such as the ingredients needed, the quantities of each ingredient, the cooking time and temperature, and any special instructions or tips to ensure a successful result. What I am trying to achieve is to help young teens or adults who have no knowledge of cooking and are making their first steps to cook.
